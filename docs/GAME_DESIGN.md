## 1. Game Overview

### High Concept
"A heartwarming 2D side-scrolling adventure where toddler Heraniya travels through her home to visit her baby brother Somdev, with simple one-tap controls perfect for very young players."

### Genre
- **Primary:** Casual Platformer
- **Secondary:** Educational/Family
- **Style:** Mario-inspired endless runner with checkpoints

### Platform
- **Primary:** Mobile (iOS 13+, Android 8+)
- **Tablet optimized:** iPad, Android tablets
- **Secondary:** Web browser (HTML5)

### Game Length
- **Single Playthrough:** 8-12 minutes
- **Replay Value:** High (vehicle choices, collection completion)
- **Session Length:** 5-15 minutes ideal

## 2. Core Design Pillars

### Simplicity
- One-tap controls throughout
- Clear visual language
- Immediate feedback on all actions
- No complex menus or options

### Safety
- No fail states or "game over"
- No scary content or loud sounds
- No in-app purchases (base version)
- No advertisements
- Offline playable

### Emotional Connection
- Real family members as characters
- Positive reinforcement only
- Celebration of sibling love
- Familiar home environment

### Replayability
- Multiple vehicle choices
- Different collectible paths
- Photo moments to capture
- Daily visit motivation

### Accessibility
- High contrast visuals
- Adjustable audio levels
- Baby-safe mode (muted)
- Colorblind-friendly design
